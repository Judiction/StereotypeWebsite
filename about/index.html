About

Hi, I'm Judd. I'm a 3D animator and creative technologist based in Rio de Janeiro, currently working at NOO Works. With a Bachelor’s degree in Film Production from Université de Montréal, my work blends cinematic storytelling with emerging technologies, interactive media, and experimental digital aesthetics. My practice throughout the past 10 years spans 3D animation, motion design, generative art, and creative coding, constantly exploring new visual languages and technological frontiers. Driven by curiosity and innovation, I create immersive experiences that merge art, design, and technology.
